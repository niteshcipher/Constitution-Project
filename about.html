About Nagrik Aur Samvidhan

Our mission is to empower citizens by spreading knowledge of the Indian Constitution through engaging and accessible learning tools.

Our Vision

We envision an India where every citizen is aware of their constitutional rights and responsibilities and plays an active role in the democratic process.

Our Team

We are a group of educators, developers, and legal experts dedicated to promoting constitutional literacy in fun and interactive ways.

Preamble

The Preamble serves as an introduction to the Constitution and states its objectives: Justice, Liberty, Equality, and Fraternity. It reflects the core values that India upholds as a democratic republic.

Fundamental Rights

The six fundamental rights include Right to Equality, Right to Freedom, Right Against Exploitation, Right to Freedom of Religion, Cultural and Educational Rights, and Right to Constitutional Remedies.

Fundamental Duties

Fundamental Duties include respecting the national symbols, protecting public property, and upholding the spirit of unity and integrity in the country.

Directive Principles

The Directive Principles of State Policy guide the government to create policies that provide social and economic justice and aim at the welfare of the people.
